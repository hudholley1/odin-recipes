Eggplant Parmigiana Recipe

A delicious baked-layering of eggplant, basil, pecorino romano, mozzarella, and delicate tomate sauce

Ingredients:

  • Tomato sauce
  • pecorino romano
  • basil
  • eggplant
  • breadcrumbs
  • flour

Steps:

  1. Preheat oven to 150.
  2. Saauce it up.
  3. Slice eggplants
  4. Bread em
  5. Bake em
  6. eat it up!
